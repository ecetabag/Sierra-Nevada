Forest Management and Wildfire Control in the Sierra Nevada

By Albert Ponferrada Sicart

This website analyzes the development of forest management in the Sierra Nevada, emphasizing the influence of previous practices on the present wildfire environment. The goal is to examine the transition to new strategies, such as prescribed burns, while addressing the technical, ecological, and social obstacles encountered by forest managers in executing these approaches.

Timeline

Historical Practices and Their Results

The influence of historical forest management practices on the present condition of the Sierra Nevada's forests.

Modern Strategies for Wildfire Mitigation

Contemporary strategies designed to mitigate wildfire risks and rehabilitate forest ecosystems in the Sierra Nevada.

Climate Change and Future Challenges

The impact of climate change and other current conditions on wildfire behavior and forest management in the Sierra Nevada.
